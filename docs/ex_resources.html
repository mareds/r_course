Packages

You should have the following packages installed on your computer to follow the exercises and demonstrations in the lectures (unless you will work on the instituteâ€™s R Studio Server)

  • tidyverse (includes already: ggplot2, tibble, tidyr, readr, dplyr, stringr, forcats, purrr)
  • lubridate
  • modelr
  • ggthemes
  • RColorBrewer
  • ggridges
  • ggmap
  • grid
  • gridExtra
  • cowplot
  • FSA

All these packages are available on CRAN and can be installed in one go using the install.packages() function:

install.packages(c("tidyverse", "lubridate", "modelr", "ggthemes", "RColorBrewer", "ggmap", 
  "grid", "gridExtra", "cowplot", "FSA"))
